Online Mini-course: Convergence of Riemannian Manifolds with Lower Scalar Curvature Bounds

Time: November 3, 2025 --- November 14, 2025
https://geometry-topology.github.io/course-convergence.html



Overview
  • This is a two weeks long course about the convergence of Riemannian manifolds with lowe scalar curvature bounds.

Syllabus

Week 1

  1. Preliminary: Sobolev and Neumann constants, Gromov-Hausdorff convergence, Convergence in the Dong-Song sense, Stable systoles, Harmonic representation of integral forms
  2. Scalar Curvature and Related Theorems

Week 2

  1. Torus rigidity theorem
  2. Stern's inequality
  3. Positive mass theorem rigidity using BKKS mass inequality
  4. Dong-Song's Stability of Positive Mass Theorem
  5. Stability of Three-tori with small negative scalar curvature
