Geometry and Topology Seminar

This seminar explores a variety of topics and challenges in fields such as Quantitative Topology, Metric Topology, Simplicial Volume and Bounded Cohomology, Geometric Topology, and Hyperbolic Manifolds. If you are interested in presenting a talk, please reach out to lizhi.chen.math@gmail.com .

Talks are conducted either in person (with hybrid options) or online, as indicated. In-person sessions are currently hosted at Lanzhou University, China, while online talks are scheduled in China Standard Time (GMT+8).
