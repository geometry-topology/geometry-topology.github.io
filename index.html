Geometry and Topology Seminar


This seminar aims at topics in the areas of Quantitative Topology and Quantitative Geometry, Metric Topology and Metric Geometry, Simplicial Volume and Bounded Cohomology, Topological Complexity, Hyperbolic Geometry, Geometric Topology, Coarse Geometry and K-theory, Differential Geometry and related topics.

Talks are conducted either in person (with hybrid options) or online, as indicated. In-person sessions are usually scheduled on Friday, while online talks are scheduled in China Standard Time (GMT+8). Usually talks are recorded. Videos will be made available online upon approval of the speakers. If you are interested in presenting a talk, please send a message to lizhi.chen.math@gmail.com (lizhi (dot) chen (dot) math (at) gmail (dot) com).

If you wish to be on the seminar mailing list and receive announcements, please send an email to geometrytopology2024@gmail.com (geometrytopology2024 (at) gmail (dot) com). Or if you wish to be removed from the mailing list, please send an email to the same address.

Organizers: Lizhi Chen (Lanzhou University), Bo Zhu (Texas A&M University).
