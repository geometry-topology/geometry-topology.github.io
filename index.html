Geometry and Topology Seminar

This seminar aims at various topics and problems in the following areas: geometric topology, quantitative topology, simplicial volume and bounded cohomology, etc. We will very welcome if you are interested to give a talk. Please contact lizhi.chen.math@gmail.com if you have interests to this seminar.

The talk is in-person (hybrid) or online as indicated. Currently the in-person talk is given at Lanzhou University (China). Time zone for the online talk is China Standard Time (=GMT+8).
